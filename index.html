Instructions

×
  • Guess the word in six tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the correct word.
  • If the tile is green, the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  • If the tile is yellow, the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • If the tile is gray, the letter is not in the word at all.
